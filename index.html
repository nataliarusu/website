FOUNDERS AND CODERS

Hello

My name is Natalia. I live in Cranham Upminster, a village east of Greater London. Actually, I'm from Moldova. My family and I moved to the UK in 2017. Sometimes thinking about your life in the future is more important than living in the present. Undoubtedly, it was one of the best decisions we have ever made.

As soon as we moved to the UK, I started ESOL courses. At the same time, I studied bookkeeping. Although after a year of studying bookkeeping I passed the exam with a distinction grade, I was not carried away in this area. I was advised by a relative not to waste time on bookkeeping and try to find myself in programming. It opened a whole new world for me. I plunged into the limitless world of programming. You know, I just love it.

I took Udemy courses CSS and JavaScript, learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript through freecodecamp. While taking these courses, I realized that self-study is good but not enough.

The line between learning to code and getting paid as a profession is not an easy line to cross.

Founders and Coders is an opportunity to develop specific skills that will help in my career. The real programming skills and collaborative approach that FaC teaches are very important to me and I really need them. I hope to be a part of a passionate community and I believe this course could give me a great head start in my career, providing me with opportunities in the future.
